FAQs

1. Do TV backlights really help reduce eye strain?

Yes, especially in dark rooms. TV backlights help reduce the contrast between the bright screen and a dark environment, minimizing strain on your eyes during extended viewing sessions.

2. Can I use these backlights with streaming apps on my smart TV?

Only some systems, like those with external cameras (e.g., Govee T2 or Ambivision), can sync with native smart TV apps. HDMI-based systems like Lytmi Neo require external devices such as Fire Stick, Apple TV, or gaming consoles for full functionality.

3. What’s the difference between RGB and RGBIC backlights?

RGB backlights can display one color at a time, while RGBIC (Independent Control) allows multiple colors to appear on a single strip simultaneously, enabling advanced effects and smooth gradients.

4. Do I need Wi-Fi to use smart TV backlights?

Most advanced backlight systems do require a Wi-Fi connection to access features like app control, syncing, and voice assistant integration. However, some basic LED strips can run without Wi-Fi using IR remotes or Bluetooth.
